Welcome

Xin chào from
Hood Canal

We're Linh & Trang. YuRu isn't a business — it's an invitation. A place we built to share this quiet corner of the canal with the family and friends who need to exhale.

ゆる [Yu-Ru] is a Japanese word for a relaxed, carefree vibe. That's the feeling we hope you carry home.

We keep things grounded and eco-minded here: private well water, real dishes over disposables, and a genuine care for the land we're lucky enough to call our own.

Experiences

Some moments are worth planning around. We offer a small handful of guided experiences for those who want them — a private chef sunset dinner on the water, or a chartered fishing and crabbing trip on the canal. Nothing packaged. Everything personal.

A note on eco

Leave it better than you found it

Limited refrigeration on-site — plan ahead with coolers. Well water is pure and delicious straight from the tap. All faucets, all sinks. Bring a refillable bottle and skip the plastic.

Why we exist
